About Us

At All American Meat Exporters, we are building a network of certified and professional farms equipped with the latest technology and safety protocols to provide the global market with fresh and healthy food products. We are leading the market in innovation with our meat and rice based products. In our effort to continue innovating and disrupting the global market, we are continually striving to better ourselves and introduce new products based on international standards. Our customers rely on us to find the products with best quality and value. We believe our first responsibility is to our consumers and our customers.

We bring local cultures, to global markets

We protect our clients’ interests by adapting to changing market dynamics and becoming the leaders in innovating in the food industry. We closely observe market trends, consumer behaviour to continually innovate in our products and forecast business. With our brand ‘Dokea’, we plan to bring our local culture to global markets all the while maintaining a local market presence in all countries we export our products to. Our diverse staff is fluent In local cultures, customs and traditions and is well equipped to handle the changing dynamics of the global markets. We know what is most important to our customers in their own markets, through personalised requirements, we deliver quality and reliability to our clientele.

Here at All American Meat exporter for over a decade, we have focused on one goal - building our clients' success and profitability while becoming the leaders in food safety and sustainable growth.
