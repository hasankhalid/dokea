Our Philosophy

Our mission

At All American meat exporters, our mission is to provide local organic farms with the tools, technology and skills to provide healthy and sustainable food products to the global market. Our responsibility is towards our clients the wellbeing of our farmers. Experimentation and innovation is in our DNA. We continue to expand and improve our product base and intend to lead the way to a sustainable and healthy food future.

Our Vision

At All American meat exporters. Our vision is to create a natural, sustainable and climate neutral future in the meat and food industry. To that end, we are continually investing in cutting edge research to enable our farmers to create healthy and reliable food products for the global market. We intend to connect our local culture and products with the global industry while leading the way in proposing innovative food solutions and investing in the future.
