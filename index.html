What is Dokea?

Dokea means from the hamlets in Punjabi, hamlet being a symbol of purity and quality in our culture. Dokea connects organic food producers from the hamlets of Pakistan to consumer markets across the globe. Upholding the values of freshness, purity and quality of our villages, Dokea provides an array of safe, healthy and Halal food products to our customers across the world so they can enjoy their meals with full confidence.
