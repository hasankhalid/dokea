What is Dokea?

Dokea means from the hamlets in Punjabi, hamlet being a symbol of purity and quality in Punjabi culture. Our Brand ‘Dokea’ connects organic meat producers from the hamlets of Pakistan to consumer markets across the globe. Upholding the values of freshness, purity and quality of our villages, Dokea provides an array of safe, healthy and Halal meat products to our customers across the world.
